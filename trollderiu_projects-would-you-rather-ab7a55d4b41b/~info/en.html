Create a new Poll

• From google play webpage.

• Directly on the website /.

Once inside the App, Fill a creation formulary:

• Write the title. You can add a sub-title on next line of the title textarea.
• Add options to the poll. Put every option on a different line.
• Insert your Name (optional). Can also add links for referencing this votation.
• Click on Create button.

Save and Share:

• Vote: Choose your votation. You will be able to change it in future.
• Choose from other available options. As allowing more than one vote per person.
• Make it Públic or not (only with premium version).
• Share it. On mobile devices, a window displays the different social Apps to share the image poll and link.
• Send. Make sure the image is displayed correctly and press send.
